Experience with Technology and Entrepreneurship

I am an enthusiast of technology and innovation, with a special interest in programming and entrepreneurship. I have participated in several innovation marathons, hackathons, and events focused on technology and artificial intelligence, such as the Campus Party Nordeste and the Trakto Show. My involvement with the startup ecosystem has allowed me to develop essential communication and public speaking skills, such as practicing the "Pitch", in addition to expanding my vision of different areas.

Academic Background and Olympiads

During my school journey, I participated and was awarded in several knowledge olympiads, such as: OBMEP, OBI, Mandacaru de Matemática, OCE, among others. These competitions were fundamental in consolidating my mathematical and logical foundation, essential for my evolution in programming.

In 2023, I joined a center of excellence in mathematics and programming at the Federal University of Alagoas: Novo Ensino Suplementar. This environment was crucial for my passion for programming, allowing me to interact with people who share the same desire for evolution, in addition to learning from renowned professionals from all over Brazil and the world.

Technical Skills and Goals

Currently, I consider myself an aspiring programmer. I am not a specialist, but I have determination and a desire to improve every day, learning from my mistakes and successes. I always seek to explore new tools and delve into the topics that most interest me.
